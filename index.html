💕 In Love
《书窗情缘》
——致 Sarah
波特兰街雨如纱，旧书扉页遇君家。
聂鲁达诗藏暗语，萨拉眸底落星华。
詹姆士指轻翻页，共许余生一盏茶。
红毯铺时云散尽，伞边犹系去年花。
✦ ✦ ✦
In Portland's rain, a veil of grey,
We met where worn-out love poems lay.
Neruda's lines spoke what we dared not say,
Sarah's eyes held stars at bay.
James turned pages soft and slow,
Vowing futures, sip by sip, aglow.
The aisle cleared clouds, the sun broke free,
Our umbrella still keeps last year's plea.
🌏 Long Distance
《诗笺寄远》
——致南溪
墨尔本暖京都寒，万里屏前共晓残。
昼寄晴光卿处晒，夜分雪色我窗看。
信纸千叠藏朝暮，日历一页减团圆。
待到重逢执手日，春风不用寄诗笺。
✦ ✦ ✦
Melbourne warm while Beijing cold,
Across the screen our dawns unfold.
I send you sunshine from my day,
You share the snow where night holds sway.
A thousand notes fill up the year,
Each calendar page brings you near.
When spring arrives and we embrace,
No more poems shall take your place.
